<p>The <i>QuranApp</i> facilitates reading the Qur’an on daily basis at any time and place. Its easy-to-navigate and neat and clean design helps users to overcome the difficulties of reading the Qur’an in the time of technology. This app has 15+ translations in several languages so that people of different languages and regions can understand the purpose of the Qur’an.</p><p><b>Features:</b></p><ul><li>Reading in translation mode</li><li>Reading in page mode</li><li>Read Qur’an in different translation modes (single verse, verse range, full chapter, full juz)</li><li>Convenient navigation in single verse mode</li><li>Fast jump to any verse</li><li>Fast jump to any chapter</li><li>Advanced sharing</li><li>Multiple fonts</li><li>15+ Translations<ul><li>Read multiple translations together</li><li>Fast preview of footnotes</li><li>Fast preview of verse references</li></ul></li><li>Multiple recitations<ul><li>Repeat single verse</li><li>Play verse range</li><li>Play recitation continuously</li><li>Automatic scroll to match current verse</li></ul></li><li>Verse of the Day Reminder</li><li>Customize Arabic text size</li><li>Customise translation text size</li><li>Topics from the Qur’an<ul><li>Duas from the Qur’an</li><li>Prophets mentioned in the Qur’an</li></ul></li><li>Bookmark verses for later<ul><li>Bookmark single verse</li><li>Bookmark a verse range</li><li>Add custom notes to bookmarks</li></ul></li><li>Advanced search<ul><li>Voice search</li><li>Search history</li><li>Multi page search</li><li>Search in single translation or multiple translations</li><li>Search for full word or word part</li></ul></li><li>Dark and light themes</li><li>Multiple language support</li></ul>
